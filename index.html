CHAD CARROLL

Chad Charroll is an area "Power Broker" in every sense. With a wealth of experience in real estate, finance, contracts, and negotiation, Chad skillfully structures deals designed to put clients in their dream homes and provides expert guidance by utilizing an abundance of professional resources. A frequent contributor in The Wall Street Journal, New York Times, Daily News, and The Real Deal, Chad has set an unparalleled standard among his peers.

As a self-made real estate mogul, Chad relocated from New York to Miami to make his South Florida real estate debut in 2008, shortly after the Subprime Mortgage Crisis. He quickly rose to the top 1% of agents and has consistently ranked in the top 100 agents nationally since 2011, boasting over $2B in career sales.

Always pushing forward and on the cutting-edge of innovation, Chad leverages a variety of tactics in his sales and marketing strategies from targeting international databases to find hard-to-reach buyers, to partnering with celebrities and affluent influencers.

In a world and industry that is increasingly digital yet firmly rooted in tradition, Chad knows how to balance traditional media with cutting-edge digital technology like programmatic advertising, geofencing, and artificial intelligence.

Without a doubt, Chad’s success is the product of his unremitting work ethic, personal mission, and top-notch professional service, which is only further complimented and amplified with Compass’ technology, programs, and nationwide reach.
